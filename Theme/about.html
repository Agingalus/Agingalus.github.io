About The Author

By Aaron Greene

My name is Aaron Greene. I have been started jggling about nine years ago. Since then I have never stopped. Juggling has become my true passion. It started with just regular juggling balls, but that was not enough. Thought the years I moved on to many other things including: clubs, knives, unicycles, poi, spinning staff, dragon staff, and most recently rope dart. Each one of these has its own unique style, and depending on my mood I will free swich bettween them. There is so much to learn in each one of these that I will never truely master any of them. All I can do is practice as much as I can, and have fun on the journy.
