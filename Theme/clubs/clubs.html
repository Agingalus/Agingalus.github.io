Juggling Clubs

The longest any one has ever juggled seven clubs continuously was only for 4 minutes 23 seconds!

Juggling clubs are dynamicly different from balls. Although they are usualy the next stepping stones after balls, they will probobly take more time to learn than learning balls did originaly. It is the same steps as learning balls, but unlike balls, you never have to worry about the rotaion. With balls no matter how high you throw it you can catch it with out a problem, with clubs there is only one right side. This same thing also gives clubs a completely differet look. With clubs throwing it higher and making it do more rotations is a trick on its own. Even the most basic trick looks much more impressive with clubs.
